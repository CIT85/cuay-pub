Shrimp Pad Thai

A Fusion of Flavors

Indulge in the irresistible blend of sweet, sour, and savory flavors with our Shrimp Pad Thai. This easy-to-make recipe is perfect for a quick and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup peanuts, crushed
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

How to Cook

Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. In a wok or large pan, stir-fry shrimp until pink. Push shrimp to the side and pour beaten eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs and mix with shrimp. Add cooked noodles, bean sprouts, bell pepper, and green onions. Pour Pad Thai sauce over the mixture and toss until well combined. Serve hot, garnished with crushed peanuts, cilantro, and lime wedges.
