Penne Arrabbiata

Spicy and Flavorful

Try this classic Italian Penne Arrabbiata for a deliciously spicy and flavorful pasta experience.

Ingredients

What You Will Need

Quantity Ingredient
400g penne pasta
2 cups tomato sauce
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tsp red pepper flakes
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté minced garlic in olive oil until golden.
  3. Add tomato sauce, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Toss cooked penne pasta in the spicy sauce. Serve hot.
