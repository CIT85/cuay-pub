Spaghetti

Simple Yet Satisfying

Classic and simple, this spaghetti recipe is a go-to for a quick and delicious meal.

Tasty Good

Items needed:

Quantity Ingredient
400g spaghetti
2 cups tomato sauce
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté minced garlic in olive oil until golden.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Toss cooked spaghetti in the sauce. Serve hot.

Fettuccine Alfredo

Creamy and Elegant

A rich and creamy Alfredo sauce paired with fettuccine noodles, a decadent classic.

What You Will Need

Quantity Ingredient
300g fettuccine noodles
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
to taste Salt and nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter, add heavy cream, and simmer.
  3. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted.
  4. Season with salt and nutmeg.
  5. Toss cooked fettuccine in the sauce. Serve immediately.

Classic Beef Lasagna

Meaty and Tasty

This hearty lasagna is layered with savory beef, cheese, and pasta for a comforting meal.

Delicious

Items needed:

Quantity Ingredient
1 lb ground beef
1 onion, diced
2 garlic, minced
2 cups ricotta cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella
1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
12 lasagna noodles, cooked
to taste Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning
  1. In a skillet, brown ground beef with diced onions and minced garlic.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes and seasonings, simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. In a baking dish, layer cooked lasagna noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  4. Repeat layers.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes.
  6. Let it rest before serving.
