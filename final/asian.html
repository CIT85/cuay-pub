Chicken Teriyaki Stir-Fry

Quick and Flavorful

A quick and flavorful chicken teriyaki stir-fry that's perfect for busy weeknights.

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Stir-fry sliced chicken in vegetable oil until browned. Add broccoli and bell pepper, stir-frying until vegetables are tender-crisp. In a bowl, whisk together teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, honey, and minced garlic. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables, toss to coat. Continue cooking until the sauce thickens. Serve over rice, garnished with sesame seeds.

Korean Mixed Rice Bowl (Bibimbap)

Bowl of Deliciousness

Bibimbap is a delicious Korean dish that features a colorful array of vegetables, a protein of your choice, and a flavorful gochujang (red chili paste) sauce.

Quantity Ingredient
2 cups cooked short-grain rice
1 cup julienned carrots, sauteed
1 cup spinach, blanched
1 cup bean sprouts, blanched
1 cup sliced shiitake mushrooms, sautéed
1 cup thinly sliced beef or tofu, cooked
Mix 3 tablespoons gochujang, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 1 tablespoon sesame oil Gochujang sauce
For garnish Sesame seeds and sliced green onions
  1. Arrange the cooked rice in a bowl.
  2. Top with portions of each vegetable and the protein of your choice.
  3. Place a fried egg on top if desired.
  4. Drizzle the gochujang sauce over the bowl.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions.
  6. Mix everything together before eating to enjoy the combination of flavors.
